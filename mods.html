Modpack downloader

This site lets you download the newest versions of mods from Modrinth. Just find the modslug for the mods you want in your modpack. The modslug is at the end of the mod's Modrinth page URL. For example, for Sodium, the modslug is "sodium": https://modrinth.com/mod/sodium. Enter the modslugs into the "Mods" text field example: sodium, lithium. You can add the modslugs to the link like this: https://oc4t.github.io/modc/mods.html?mods=sodium,lithium.
